How We're Different

CADpanion isn't just a task automation tool—it's a contextual learning assistant built specifically for CAD/CAM users. While most tools focus on doing the work for you, CADpanion focuses on teaching by offering real-time, step-by-step guidance based on your design activity. It uses fine-tuned large language models and integrates directly into your CAD environment, providing explanations of features, design principles, and tool interactions as you work.

By interpreting user intent and offering targeted, in-context help, CADpanion bridges the gap between documentation and hands-on learning. It's especially useful for students, educators, and early-career engineers who want to build lasting design intuition—not just finish a task. Our system is modular, adaptable to different CAD platforms, and designed to align with real-world engineering workflows.
