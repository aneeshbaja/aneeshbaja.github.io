Campanion

Your AI Teaching Companion

Campanion is an AI-powered education platform designed to transform teachers into mentors by automating administrative grunt work. At its core, Campanion features a dynamic dashboard that tracks student progress, pushes assignments, and delivers actionable insights — all while giving educators the tools to focus on what truly matters: teaching.

Our modular plugin system empowers schools to tailor the platform to their curriculum, with intelligent assistants for CAD (engineering design), coding, business, physics, and more. Whether it's generating personalized feedback, supporting students in real time, or integrating seamlessly with LMS platforms like Canvas or Moodle, Campanion is your all-in-one AI teaching companion — like having a Jarvis for the classroom.
